Data Sources & Citations
O*NET Production Database 28.0
Occupation backbone. SOC-coded job titles, skills, task and ability profiles used to anchor exposure scores across 1,100+ occupations, aggregated to 80 representative groups.
onetcenter.org/database.html
Felten, Raj & Seamans AIOE (2021)
AI Occupational Exposure index. Maps O*NET ability ratings against AI Progress Measurement (AIPM) benchmark scores. Published in AEA Papers & Proceedings, May 2021.
aeaweb.org · 10.1257/pandp.20211018
BLS Employment Projections 2022–32
Official 10-year projections: employment levels, percent change, and job openings by occupation. Used to weight risk by workforce size and display labor-force exposure.
bls.gov/emp/
Goldman Sachs Research (2023)
Briggs & Kodnani: "The Potentially Large Effects of AI on Economic Growth." Calibrates S-curve adoption scenario parameters; estimates 300M jobs globally exposed to generative AI.
goldmansachs.com/intelligence
Impact Score Formula: score(job, t) = aioe_baseline × L / (1 + exp(−k × (t − t₀))) × 100
Scenarios — Conservative: L=0.55, k=0.35, t₀=7.0  |  Base: L=0.85, k=0.55, t₀=5.5  |  Aggressive: L=1.0, k=0.80, t₀=3.5. t ∈ [0,10] = years since 2020. AIOE baselines from Felten–Raj–Seamans (2021), employment-weighted by BLS major-group crosswalk. These are model-based scenarios, not forecasts.
